Hand Coded

My services are unique and unlike other web development businesses because all my websites are hand coded. You will often see the word 'Wordpress' thrown around a lot; Wordpress and other page builders are hailed by web development agencies because it makes their life easy but it actually comes with a host of issues:

They are convoluted: A hand coded website will load often load 10 times faster than a wordpress site.

They are not optimised for mobile devices: this is a major issue considering Google ranks mobile friendly websites much higher.

Wordpress sites are riddled with security vulnerabilities allowing hackers to gain sensitive data, static hand coded websites are impenetrable as there is no access points for hackers.

Wordpress sites require constant updates and attention as plugins go out of date.
